Lyrcs

Song Writing Has Never Been Easier.

Lyrcs' mission is to be the single tool that empowers you to do what you do best - tell stories through music. Made for songwriters, by songwriters.

Features You'll Love

Bundled Rhyme Dictionary

Gone are the days of opening a browser and checking rhymezone to find that perfect rhyme.

  • Group by syllables or letters
  • Copy to clipboard or insert directly
  • No internet required

Instant Syllable Counting

Gone are the days of manually counting on your fingers.

  • Syllable count per line
  • Syllable count of selected text (right click)
  • Override syllable count of words (right click or preferences)
  • No internet required

Beautiful Design

Working late at night? Eyesight isn't what it used to be? Wish you could work without distractions? We got you.

  • Minimalistic UI
  • Dark mode support
  • Dark/light mode override (preferences)
  • Adjustable Font (CMD +, CMD -)
  • Tab support (CMD T)

Native macOS Integration

Everything you come to expect from a great macOS app is included.

  • Spelling and grammar checking (right click)
  • Dictation (Edit > Start Dictation)
  • Share (File > Share)
  • Undo/Redo

and much more!
